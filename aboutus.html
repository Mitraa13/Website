ABOUT THE CHENNAI CAMPUS

Situated in the sprawling & modern New Vision Township, off the Vandalur-Kelambakkam Road, FIITJEE Global School Chennai (Vengambakkam) Campus is an architectural marvel. Spanning a land area of 3.34 acres and a built-up area of 7576.34 square meters it hosts unmatched facilities for a complete schooling experience. Being the first campus of the FIITJEE Global School chain it serves as the mother centre and role model for other campuses of the brand.

It is surrounded by open spaces and there is a large waterbody in the vicinity where birds of myriad species abound. Residential villas and service apartments dot the adjoining area with gated community living. All conceivable amenities, right from assembly area to auditorium, labs, library, cafeteria are available within the Vengambakkam campus. There are two huge basketball courts, one synthetic tennis court, one volleyball court along with two cricket practice net set-ups. Apart from these outdoor sports facilities’ indoor games like badminton, chess, table-tennis are also provisioned in the school. The campus conforms to the ecological sustainability goals by incorporating rain-water harvesting and tapping solar power. Usage of plastic is prohibited within the school premises.

ABOUT THE VELLORE CAMPUS

Nestled in the lap of nature, the FIITJEE Global School Vellore (Katpadi) Campus is situated within the expansive & contemporary Township of Sameera Garden City, in Katpadi. Vellore Campus is an artistic masterpiece & architectural marvel. Spanning a land area of 4.21 acres and a built-up area of 7413 square meters it hosts unmatched facilities for a complete schooling experience. Being the second campus of the FIITJEE Global School chain it serves as one of the nodal centres for other campuses of the brand.

It is surrounded by mountains and lush greenery in ambient vicinity. Residential villas and service apartments dot the adjoining area with gated community living. All conceivable amenities, right from assembly area to auditorium, labs, library, cafeteria are available within the Vellore campus. There are two badminton courts, one tennis court, one volleyball court, one basketball court, one squash court along with two cricket practice net set-ups. Apart from these outdoor sports facilities’ indoor games like chess, table-tennis are also provisioned in the school. The campus conforms to the ecological sustainability goals by incorporating rain-water harvesting and tapping solar power. Usage of plastic is prohibited within the school premises.

MISSION, VISION & CORE VALUES

Mission

To transform every child to unravel their optimum potential. To transform the Indian Society with humans optimally fit – mentally, physically & spiritually. To reach farthest corners of the country in the next 20 years to achieve our Mission.

Vision

School Education has the single biggest effect on a country’s GDP i.e. prosperity & development. FFERT is dedicated to be a dominant player in this quest.

For a country’s growth & development, it is important that citizens understand its rich culture, & heritage and are proud of it. Indian culture, our ancient education system, our Vedas, scriptures are the supreme source of knowledge and embody the highest education principles. Their contribution to the modern advances in the field of sciences, engineering, medicine, humanities etc. are still being discovered.

Our vision is to impart such education to students so that they acquire wisdom from this ancient legacy and develop their skills from modern-day developments. We aim to blend the best of both ancient & modern.

Core Values

Our Core values support our vision. They shape our culture, and reflect what our institution values the most. These are our School’s principles, beliefs and values.

Cultural Awareness Creativity & Innovation Balanced Mind & Body
Hardwork & Discipline High Quality Standards Continual Learning
